A blue scan line sweeps top-to-bottom across the screen. Above the line: CRM fades out. Below the line: globe materializes. Like a radar sweep activating the map. ~500ms, smoother, still premium.

CRM slides out left, Market Intelligence slides in from right — like flipping a page. Familiar spatial metaphor, very fast at ~350ms. Less dramatic but feels snappy and purposeful. Easier to reverse-animate when switching back.
