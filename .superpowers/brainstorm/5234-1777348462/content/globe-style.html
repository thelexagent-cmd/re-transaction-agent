Globe Visual Style

What should the 3D globe actually look like?

WIREFRAME

A — Blueprint Grid

Pure lat/lon wireframe on a dark sphere. Technical, architectural feel — like a mission control display. Blue grid lines, glowing ZIP markers. No texture, all data. Think SpaceX launch deck.

PARTICLE CLOUD

B — Particle Cloud

Thousands of tiny dots outline the continents, glowing brighter where data exists. More organic than wireframe — feels alive, like a breathing data constellation. Indigo/violet palette.

DARK EARTH

C — Dark Earth

Dark continent silhouettes on a near-black ocean, with faint city-light dots and glowing ZIP markers with pulse rings. Most realistic/premium feel. Bloomberg Terminal meets NASA night-lights. Needs a real texture map or SVG paths for production.

This sets the entire visual personality of the Market Intelligence view.
